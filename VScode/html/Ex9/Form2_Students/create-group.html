SpTH Spain Travel Health

To create your family/group FCS you must be of legal age.

Once you have filled in the data, you will receive an e-mail in your e-mail account, with a security code and a link to create the group/family form. In this form you will have to indicate your travel details and the details of all the passengers who will travel with you. Once you have filled in the data, you will receive a QR code for each of the passengers in the group.

Remember that it is mandatory to complete this form for all passengers entering Spain from any country and that each form is associated with a single trip, is personal and non-transferable.

Your personal data will be processed in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of their personal data and on the free movement of such data and Organic Law 3/2018 of 5 December on the Protection of Personal Data and Guarantee of Digital Rights and other related regulations.

The data controller will be the Ministry of Health (and other health authorities). For more information you can see the section on data processing.

YOU SHOULD NOT TRAVEL if you have symptoms compatible with COVID-19 (fever, cough, respiratory distress), recent onset, if you have been diagnosed with COVID-19 in the last 14 days or if you have had close contact with a confirmed case of COVID-19 in the last two weeks.

Legal Information

Each passenger must complete a form associated with each trip, which is personal and non-transferable. Make sure you fill out this form and sign it before flying to Spain. And remember that, after signing the FCS form, you will receive a QR code associated with your trip, which you must bring on your cell phone or printed on paper in order to pass the airport health control.

Likewise, when passing through the airport control, you may be subjected to a temperature control by means of non-contact thermometers or thermographic cameras. These tests will be carried out to guarantee the passenger's privacy and the data obtained will not be stored.

Who is responsible for the processing of your data?
The data controller "SpTH Spain Travel Health" (https://www.spth.gob.es/) is the Spanish Ministry of Health.
What is the purpose of the processing of your data?
Epidemiological surveillance and health control for the pandemic caused by COVID-19. To guarantee the provision of ordinary or extraordinary services necessary for the sanitary control of the entry of passengers coming from any country. Statistical uses and sanitary research.
What is the applicable legal basis?
The main legal basis that legitimizes the processing "SpTH Spain Travel Health" is art. 6.1.e) of the General Data Protection Regulation (RGPD/GDPR): "The processing is necessary for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest or in the exercise of official authority vested in the controller".
To which area is your data communicated?
Health Areas and Departments of the Spanish Government Delegations and Subdelegations, Health Authorities of the Autonomous Communities, as well as other health bodies or administrations and health authorities of other countries.
What are your rights when you provide us with your data?
You can exercise, among others, the rights of: access, cancellation, deletion, limitation, rectification, portability and right to be forgotten insofar as the legal obligations allow it.
Which is the data that we process?
Through this website, your personal data may be collected directly from you (by filling out the SPTH form, for example) or indirectly (through cookies).
What cookies are collected?
Technical cookies, necessary for navigation, are collected on the basis of the legitimate interest of the Data Controller (art 6. 1.f of the RGPD). An hcaptcha component of Intuition Machines, Inc, is used to provide security (confirm that access to the site is performed by a person and not a robot). The cookie does not share personal data. WAF (source IP capture) technology is also used to prevent cyber-attacks. For more information about the processing of personal data, click here.
